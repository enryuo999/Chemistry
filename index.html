Step 1: Formation of Piperidine Intermediate

REACTION TYPE
Condensation & Cyclization
WHAT HAPPENS
Reactants such as 4-chlorobenzaldehyde, formaldehyde, and ammonia condense and cyclize to form a substituted piperidine ring.

Reaction Schema

4-chlorobenzaldehyde + HCHO + NH3 → Piperidine Intermediate
